# QA Not-To-Do

Here, in no particular order, are the things I’ve been trying to avoid as a Quality Assurance:

- Make assumptions without validation. 
- No documents. 
- No expectations. 
- No patience.
- No priority.
- No scope. 
- Repeat myself. 
- Think dependently.
